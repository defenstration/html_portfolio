David's Favorite Movies

My 3 favorite movies of ALL TIME!!!

We are going to look at my favorite movies ever, genre by genre.

Comedy

The Gamers: Dorkness Rising

Dead Gentleman are an indie film company from Seattle, Washington, and while they have made several movies that I enjoy, the one that introduced me to them was The Gamers: Dorkness Rising.

Action

The A Team

While I am not old enough to have watched the series when it originally aired, The A Team movie was so much fun that I went back to watch the show (Worth It!). I mean, who doesn't want to fly a tank?

Anime

Howl's Moving Castle

Tough choice, but the pick for my favorite anime movie is Howl's Moving Castle. Studio Ghibli is awesome and this is the movie that got me started watching anime movies.
